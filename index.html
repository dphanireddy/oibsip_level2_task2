Biography

Lionel Messi, born on June 24, 1987, in Rosario, Argentina, is a professional footballer who plays as a forward. He is considered one of the greatest players of all time, known for his exceptional skills, speed, and goal-scoring ability.Lionel Messi's journey began on the streets, dribbling a ball with unmatched passion. Despite his growth deficiency, his talent shone through, leading him to FC Barcelona's La Masia academy at the tender age of 13.

Achievements

  • 7 Ballon d'Or awards
  • 6 Golden Shoes
  • Multiple Champions League titles
  • Numerous domestic league titles with Barcelona and PSG
  • Copa America 2021 winner
  • FIFA World Cup 2022 winner
